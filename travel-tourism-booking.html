Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour? +
Simply fill out our search form with your destination, travel dates, and number of travelers. Then browse through available packages and select the one that suits you best. Complete the booking process with your payment information.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are encrypted and secure.
Can I cancel or modify my booking? +
Yes! Most packages can be modified up to 48 hours before departure. Cancellation policies vary by package. Check your booking confirmation for specific terms and conditions.
Are travel documents/visas included? +
Travel insurance can be added to your booking for additional cost. Visas and travel documents are your responsibility, though we provide guidance on requirements for your destination.
What's included in the tour packages? +
Package inclusions vary. Most include accommodation, guided tours, some meals, and airport transfers. Check the detailed itinerary for each package to see exactly what's included.
Do you offer group discounts? +
Yes! Groups of 10 or more people receive special discounts. Contact our group travel specialists for a customized quote and arrangement.
